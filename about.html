DonTheMons Irie Items is a hometown silk screening operation in beautiful Carolina Beach, NC. Owner, Don Filippone has more than a decade of screen-printing experience with t-shirts, hats, stickers, skateboards and more. Don is honest and reliable and takes pride in a finished product that is done well. At DonTheMons Irie Items, we welcome emails or calls with any questions or new ideas. We can do custom work for businesses, parties, holidays, reunions, sports teams, clubs or events. The possibilities are endless. We look forward to helping you soon.
